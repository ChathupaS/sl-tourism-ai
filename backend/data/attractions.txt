SIGIRIYA ROCK FORTRESS
Location: Central Province, near Dambulla
Type: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ancient Ruins, Nature
Duration: 3-4 hours
Best for: History lovers, photography, adventure
Entry fee: $30 USD for foreigners
Highlights: 200-metre rock fortress, ancient frescoes, mirror wall, lion's paw entrance, panoramic jungle views
Nearby: Dambulla Cave Temple (20 km), Pidurangala Rock (2 km)
Best time: Early morning 6am to beat heat and crowds
Tips: Hire a guide at entrance. Avoid midday heat.

GALLE FORT
Location: Southern Province, Galle City
Type: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Colonial History, Beach
Duration: Half to full day
Best for: History, culture, photography, shopping, food
Entry fee: Free
Highlights: Dutch colonial architecture, lighthouse, rampart walks at sunset, boutique cafes, craft shops
Nearby: Unawatuna Beach (5 km), Jungle Beach (8 km)
Tips: Stay inside the fort for a unique experience. Many boutique guesthouses available.

YALA NATIONAL PARK
Location: Southern Province
Type: Wildlife Safari, Nature
Duration: Half day morning or evening safari (3-4 hours)
Best for: Wildlife lovers, photography
Entry fee: $25 USD plus jeep hire $50-80 USD
Highlights: Highest leopard density in the world, elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, hundreds of bird species
Best time: February to July (dry season, animals gather near water)
Tips: Book jeep in advance. Morning safaris give best wildlife sightings.

MIRISSA BEACH
Location: Southern Province, near Matara
Type: Beach, Whale Watching, Surfing
Duration: 1-3 days
Best for: Beach lovers, whale watching, backpackers, couples
Entry fee: Free
Highlights: Whale and dolphin watching tours (December to April), palm-fringed crescent beach, surfing, fresh seafood
Nearby: Weligama Beach (10 km, good for beginner surfing), Galle Fort (35 km)
Tips: Book whale watching tour the evening before. Arrive at beach by 6am.

TEMPLE OF THE TOOTH (DALADA MALIGAWA)
Location: Kandy, Central Province
Type: Religious Site, Cultural Heritage, UNESCO World Heritage Site
Duration: 1-2 hours
Best for: Culture, religion, history
Entry fee: LKR 1500 (about $5 USD)
Highlights: Sacred tooth relic of the Buddha, traditional Kandyan architecture, Esala Perahera festival in July and August
Tips: Dress modestly. Arrive early morning for puja ceremony.

ELLA
Location: Uva Province, hill country
Type: Nature, Hiking, Scenic Train Ride, Village
Duration: 2-3 days
Best for: Backpackers, hikers, nature lovers
Highlights: Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam's Peak hike (easy), Ella Rock hike (moderate), Ravana Falls, stunning valley views
Tips: Take the scenic train from Kandy - one of the world's most beautiful rail journeys. Book tickets in advance.

NUWARA ELIYA
Location: Central Province, highest city in Sri Lanka
Type: Nature, Tea Plantations, Colonial Hill Station
Duration: 1-2 days
Best for: Tea lovers, cooler weather, garden enthusiasts
Highlights: Tea factory tours (Pedro, Mackwoods), Gregory Lake, Horton Plains World's End day trip, colonial bungalows
Tips: Rent a tuk-tuk for the day. Visit a working tea factory for a free tour and cheap fresh tea.

TRINCOMALEE
Location: Eastern Province, northeast coast
Type: Beach, Snorkeling, Whale Watching, Hindu Temples
Duration: 2-4 days
Best for: Beach lovers, divers, those avoiding tourist crowds
Highlights: Nilaveli and Uppuveli beaches, Pigeon Island snorkeling, Koneshwaram Temple, whale and dolphin watching
Best time: May to September (east coast dry season)
Tips: Very peaceful compared to southern beaches. Great for diving.

ANURADHAPURA
Location: North Central Province
Type: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ancient Capital, Buddhist History
Duration: Full day or overnight
Best for: History, Buddhism, archaeology
Entry fee: $25 USD
Highlights: Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, Sri Maha Bodhi (oldest recorded tree in the world at 2300 years old), moonstone carvings
Tips: Rent a bicycle to get between the spread-out ruins. Dress modestly.

DAMBULLA CAVE TEMPLE
Location: Central Province
Type: UNESCO World Heritage Site, Religious Site
Duration: 1-2 hours
Best for: Culture, art, history
Entry fee: LKR 1500
Highlights: Five cave temples with 157 Buddha statues, 2000-year-old murals covering 2100 square metres
Tips: Often combined with Sigiriya day trip. Remove shoes before entering.

HORTON PLAINS AND WORLD'S END
Location: Central Province, near Nuwara Eliya
Type: Nature, Hiking, National Park
Duration: Full day
Entry fee: $15 USD
Highlights: World's End (800m sheer cliff drop), Baker's Falls, cloud forest, endemic wildlife
Tips: Depart Nuwara Eliya by 5am. Clouds cover World's End viewpoint by 9am.
