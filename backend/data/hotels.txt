BUDGET ACCOMMODATION IN SRI LANKA (under $30 per night)

Ella - Budget
- Ella Flower Garden Resort: Hostel dorms from $8/night, private rooms $20-30, great valley views
- Dream Cafe Ella: Popular backpacker spot, rooftop restaurant, dorms $6-10/night

Kandy - Budget
- McLeod Inn: Clean rooms near Temple of the Tooth, $15-25/night
- Expeditor Tourist Inn: Central location, $20-35/night

Colombo - Budget
- The Hosteller Colombo: Modern hostel, dorms $8-12, private $25-40
- Clock Inn Colombo: Budget hotel, $30-50/night

Mirissa - Budget
- Palace Mirissa: Beach-facing rooms, $25-45/night
- Hotel King Fisher: Short walk to beach, $20-35/night

Sigiriya - Budget
- Sigiriya Rest House: Basic rooms near rock fortress, $15-25/night
- Village Guest House Sigiriya: Family-run, home-cooked meals, $10-20/night

MID-RANGE ACCOMMODATION ($30-150 per night)

Galle Fort - Mid-range
- Fort Bazaar: Boutique heritage hotel in restored Dutch colonial house, $80-120/night
- The Fort Printers: Colonial boutique hotel inside the fort walls, $100-150/night

Kandy - Mid-range
- Earl's Regency: Pool, mountain views, $70-100/night
- Mahaweli Reach Hotel: Riverside hotel, $80-120/night

Nuwara Eliya - Mid-range
- Tea Factory Hotel: Unique converted tea factory experience, $100-150/night
- Grand Hotel: Colonial-era heritage hotel, $80-120/night

Trincomalee - Mid-range
- Chaaya Blu: Beachfront hotel at Uppuveli, $90-140/night
- Jungle Beach by Uga Escapes: Boutique resort, $120-180/night

Yala Area - Mid-range
- Kulu Safaris Yala: Tented camp, authentic safari experience, $100-150/night
- Cinnamon Wild Yala: Safari lodge with pool, $150-200/night

LUXURY ACCOMMODATION (above $150 per night)

- Wild Coast Tented Lodge (Yala): Award-winning eco-luxury safari lodge, $500+/night
- Amangalla (Galle Fort): Historic luxury hotel inside the fort, $300+/night
- Cape Weligama: Clifftop luxury above Weligama Bay, $400+/night
- Santani Wellness Resort (Kandy): Wellness and spa, mountain views, $350+/night
- Heritance Kandalama (near Sigiriya): Geoffrey Bawa architecture built into the rock, $200-300/night

COLOMBO CITY HOTELS
- Budget: The Hosteller, Clock Inn ($30-50/night)
- Mid-range: Tintagel Colombo ($120-180/night), Cinnamon Grand ($150-200/night)
- Luxury: Shangri-La Colombo ($250-400/night), Galle Face Hotel historic colonial ($200-350/night)
