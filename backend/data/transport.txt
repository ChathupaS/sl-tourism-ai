GETTING AROUND SRI LANKA

TRAINS (Most scenic option - highly recommended)
Sri Lanka Railways runs one of Asia's most scenic rail networks.

Key Routes:
- Colombo to Kandy: 2.5-3 hours, LKR 150-350 (2nd or 3rd class)
- Kandy to Ella: 6-7 hours scenic mountain railway through tea plantations. One of the world's most beautiful train journeys. Book observation car well in advance.
- Colombo to Anuradhapura: 4-5 hours
- Colombo to Trincomalee: 6-7 hours, departs early morning
- Colombo to Galle: 2 hours along the coast

Train Classes:
- 1st class air-conditioned: LKR 500-1500
- 2nd class: LKR 200-500, reserve a seat
- 3rd class: LKR 80-200, no seat reservation, cheaper

Booking: Book online at eRailBooking.lk or at the station. Reserve 30 days ahead for Kandy to Ella route.

BUSES (Cheapest option)
Two types available: government CTB buses (cheap, slow) and private intercity express (faster, recommended)
- Colombo to Galle: 2 hours, LKR 100-200 by express
- Colombo to Kandy: 3 hours, LKR 150-250
- Colombo to Anuradhapura: 4 hours, LKR 300-400
- Buses reach areas trains do not, such as Mirissa, Yala area and Horton Plains

TUK-TUKS (Three-wheelers, essential for short trips)
- Negotiate price before getting in or use PickMe app (Sri Lanka version of Uber)
- Short city trips: LKR 100-300
- Half day hire: LKR 1500-3000
- Great for exploring Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa and Galle Fort area

CAR HIRE WITH DRIVER (Most comfortable for multi-destination trips)
- Cost: $40-70 USD per day including driver and fuel
- Driver acts as informal guide, handles parking, knows local roads
- Best for: Families, covering cultural triangle (Sigiriya, Dambulla, Polonnaruwa, Anuradhapura) in 2-3 days
- Book through your guesthouse or hotel

DOMESTIC FLIGHTS
- Operated by Cinnamon Air and FitsAir
- Colombo to Jaffna: 50 minutes versus 8 hours by road
- Colombo to Trincomalee: 45 minutes
- Prices: $60-150 USD one way
- Useful for reaching the north or east coast quickly

TRAVEL TIMES FROM COLOMBO
- Galle: 1.5 hours by expressway or 2 hours by coastal train
- Kandy: 2.5 hours by train or car
- Sigiriya: 3.5-4 hours by car (no direct train)
- Ella: 6 hours by scenic train from Kandy or 5 hours by car from Colombo
- Yala: 5-6 hours by car (no train access)
- Trincomalee: 6 hours by train or 5 hours by car
- Jaffna: 7 hours by train or 1 hour by domestic flight

PRACTICAL TIPS
- Sri Lanka is small but roads are slow due to traffic and road quality
- Always add extra time to every journey
- Use PickMe app in Colombo for safe, metered tuk-tuks
- Night buses and trains exist but are not recommended for tourists
- East coast (Trincomalee, Arugam Bay): best reached by road. West and south: trains are great.
